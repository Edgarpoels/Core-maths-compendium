How the qualification works

The exam structure

Core Maths is 100% exam, sat at the end of the course. Everyone takes Paper 1. You then take one version of Paper 2 — your teacher chooses the option your class studies.

Paper 1 · Compulsory

Paper 1

1h 30m · 60 marks · everyone sits this

Covers the four compulsory topics: Analysis of Data, Maths for Personal Finance, Estimation, and Critical Analysis. Uses the pre-released Preliminary Material.

Paper 2 · Pick one

Paper 2A / 2B / 2C

1h 30m · 60 marks · one option only

2A Statistical techniques · 2B Critical path & risk · 2C Graphical & decision techniques. Each pairs with compulsory content from Paper 1.
